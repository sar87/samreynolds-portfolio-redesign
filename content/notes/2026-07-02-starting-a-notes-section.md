This is a new corner of the site for shorter, more frequent writing - quick thoughts, announcements, links and work-in-progress ideas that don't warrant a full publication or talk.

The format is borrowed (with thanks) from my collaborator [Anil Madhavapeddy's notes](https://anil.recoil.org/notes): a simple reverse-chronological list of dated entries, each one small enough to write in a few minutes.
