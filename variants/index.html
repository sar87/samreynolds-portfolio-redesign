Hero animation options

Alternatives to the current murmuration (boids) hero — same layout, different nature + AI animation. Move your cursor to see each react.

  1. Current — Murmuration
    A cursor-reactive starling flock (boids). Emergent flocking — many simple agents, one intelligence.
  2. Option 1 — Currents
    Particles streaming along an invisible flow field, like wind, water or migration currents shaped by a computed field. The cursor bends the flow.
    • Currents · green + amber — warm secondary; bolder contrast (amber is the site's media accent).
    • Currents · green + teal — cool secondary; harmonious emerald→aqua blend.
  3. Option 2 — Mycelium
    An organic branching network that grows and pulses with travelling signals — the fungal "wood-wide web" meets a neural network. Growth reaches toward the cursor.
  4. Option 3 — Corpus
    A drifting field of real ecology & AI terms; words flare green as they are "read". Literally "every word ever written about nature". Words part around the cursor.

More current-style backgrounds (green + amber)

Further variations on the flow-field "Currents" idea, each rendering the field differently. All use amber as the secondary colour.

  1. A — Contours
    A living topographic map: flowing iso-lines from green lowlands to amber peaks. The cursor pushes up amber elevation rings.
  2. B — Embers
    Soft glowing particles (pollen / plankton / embers) drifting on the current with additive bloom. Embers scatter and flare amber near the cursor.
  3. C — Vector field
    A structured grid of short strokes aligning to the flow, like iron filings or a wind map. The field swirls and turns amber around the cursor.

Denser & more reactive (latest round)

Round addressing two notes on the current hero: too sparse, and too weak a cursor response. All of these run ~3× the particle density (scaled to screen size) and track cursor velocity — fast movements leave a wake — with a much larger interaction radius.

  1. 1 — Currents · dense
    The current hero, intensified: ~3,500 finer particles, and the cursor now swirls the flow and drags a wake behind it instead of gently nudging.
  2. 2 — Whirlpool
    The cursor is a vortex: particles orbit and spiral inward around it, flaring white at the core. Hold still and a whirlpool forms; move and it churns.
  3. 3 — Silk
    Long persistent trails weave a dense silk sheet. Dragging the cursor combs the fibres along your motion — brighter the faster you comb.
  4. 4 — Lattice
    A different texture: ~2,500 breathing dots in a springy grid. The lattice parts instantly around the cursor and snaps back — the most immediate response of the set.
