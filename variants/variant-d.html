Conservation Evidence Copilot — demo

The Conservation Copilot

An AI assistant that lets conservation decision-makers converse directly with the evidence base — ask a question in plain language, and receive an answer grounded in the published and grey literature, with citations.

Built with the Conservation Evidence project at Cambridge, it is part of a broader effort to make evidence synthesis continuous, transparent and accessible to practitioners.
